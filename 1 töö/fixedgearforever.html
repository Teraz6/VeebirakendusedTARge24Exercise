Fixed Gear Forever: Building Your Own Bike

Building your own fixed gear bike is one of the most rewarding experiences a cycling enthusiast can have. It’s not just about assembling parts—it’s about creating something uniquely yours, a bike that reflects your style, your needs, and your passion for cycling. Whether you’re an experienced rider or a newcomer to the world of fixed gears, building your own bike allows you to learn, experiment, and customize it to perfection.

For those who want to create their bike themselves, the Veloheld frames come in three sizes and two colors and are the perfect starting point. These frames are designed for performance, durability, and aesthetics, making them an excellent choice for anyone looking to build a high-quality fixie. The versatility in sizing ensures that you’ll find a frame that fits your riding style, whether you prefer a compact and nimble design or a more relaxed, comfortable fit.

Once you’ve selected the frame, the fun begins. Start by choosing your wheels—lightweight, durable rims paired with sleek tires will make your ride smooth and fast. Then, decide on your handlebars: do you want the classic drop bars for a more aggressive position, or perhaps riser bars for a more relaxed feel? From there, you’ll move on to the drivetrain, carefully selecting a fixed gear hub and pedals that align with your preferences.

The beauty of building your own fixed gear bike is in the personalization. Every component is chosen with intention, creating a bike that feels just right. As you tighten the final bolt and spin the pedals for the first time, you’ll experience a sense of pride and satisfaction knowing you’ve built something from the ground up your own fixed gear masterpiece.
