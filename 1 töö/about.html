About Us

Welcome to Pedal Faster, the ultimate destination for cycling enthusiasts who are passionate about pushing their limits, exploring new terrains, and embracing the thrill of the ride. Whether you're a seasoned pro, a weekend warrior, or just someone discovering the joys of cycling, Pedal Faster is here to inspire, inform, and entertain.

Founded by a team of cycling aficionados, our magazine is dedicated to bringing you the latest in cycling news, in-depth gear reviews, expert tips, stunning photography, and inspiring stories from the world of cycling. We believe that cycling isn't just a sport—it's a lifestyle, a community, and a way to connect with both nature and the road.

Each issue of Pedal Faster is crafted with passion, offering everything from training advice to the latest trends in cycling culture. We dive deep into the world of racing, commuting, bikepacking, and everything in between, making sure that our content resonates with cyclists of all levels.

Join us as we celebrate the power of the pedal and the unyielding spirit of the cycling community. Whether you're chasing your next personal best, planning your dream cycling trip, or simply looking for inspiration, we’ve got you covered.

Pedal Faster—your ride starts here.
